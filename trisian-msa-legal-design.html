How Long This Lasts

Term, renewal, and when either party can end this agreement

This Agreement is a long-term partnership. We commit to managing your infrastructure, and you commit to working with us as your primary managed services provider. Here's how the timing works.

In Simple Terms

This Agreement starts on the Effective Date and runs for 36 months (three years). After that, it automatically renews unless one of you gives the other at least 90 days' notice that you don't want to renew. If rates change, we'll tell you at least 60 days in advance. If you want to leave, you have a 30-day notice period before the end of each year—after that, we move forward together for another full year.

Initial Term & Auto-Renewal

Duration: This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for thirty-six (36) months (the "Initial Term").

Auto-Renewal: At the end of the Initial Term and at the end of each renewal term thereafter, this Agreement automatically renews for successive 36-month periods on the same terms and conditions, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days before the end of the then-current term. Non-renewal notice must be sent to the Authorized Contacts listed in your Service Order.

Early Termination: Client may terminate this Agreement without cause only if:

  • Client provides at least thirty (30) days' written notice of termination; AND
  • The notice is provided within thirty (30) days before the end of Year 1, Year 2, Year 3, or any renewal term (the "Notice Window"); AND
  • Client pays any outstanding invoices and settles any early termination fees (see Section 6 for termination fees).

If notice is provided outside these Notice Windows, the Agreement continues in force and another full renewal term is triggered. There are no pro-rata refunds for early termination.

Key Dates to Mark on Your Calendar
  • Effective Date + 11 months 30 days: Last day to give notice to opt out at the end of Year 1
  • Effective Date + 23 months 30 days: Last day to give notice to opt out at the end of Year 2
  • Effective Date + 35 months 30 days: Last day to give notice to opt out at the end of Year 3

Rate Adjustments

Your Platform Fee and Support Hours rates may be adjusted from year to year to reflect market conditions, inflation, changes in Azure infrastructure costs, and Trisian's operational costs.

Notice of Adjustment: Trisian will provide notice of any rate increase at least sixty (60) days before the adjustment takes effect (typically at the start of Year 2 or a renewal term). The notice will be sent to your Authorized Contacts in writing (email is acceptable) and will include the new rates and the effective date.

Acceptance or Non-Renewal: If you do not accept the new rates, your right to terminate without penalty (beyond any early termination fee) is triggered. You must provide notice of non-renewal within the thirty (30) day Notice Window to avoid the new rates taking effect and another renewal term beginning.

You Handle

  • Data classification & handling
  • Compliance with policies
  • User training & awareness
  • ISP & carrier contracts

Key Principle: In shared items, if you don't do your part, the overall security is compromised. Example: We provide MFA, but if you don't require it, the system is still vulnerable. Work together!
